Experimental Physics (PC2193): Five lab projects requiring independent data analysis, uncertainty quantification, and comparison with theory.

  • Hall Effect in Semiconductors — Identified carrier types; cross-validated mobility through magnetoresistance (R² > 0.99); characterized extrinsic→intrinsic transition at 106°C.

Computational Work: Python and Julia simulations for quantum systems using finite difference methods and Runge-Kutta integration.

	\section{Combinations}
	We define symbol $\cmb{r}$ to represent the number of ways of choosing $r$ number of objects out of a total of $n$ number of objects.
	$$\boxed{\cmb{r}=\frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}}$$
	Here $n!$ is read as factorial $n$ and it represents the product of all natural numbers form 1 to $n$.
